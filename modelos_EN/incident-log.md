# Incident Log

This log must be completed whenever a security incident occurs, regardless of severity.  
The purpose is to maintain a history, identify patterns, and continuously improve controls.

## Guidance for Completion

**Date:**  
Day the incident was detected.

**Incident Type:**  
Examples: phishing, malware, unauthorized access, lost device, backup failure, service outage, etc.

**Description:**  
What happened? How was it detected? Which systems were affected?

**Impact:**  
- Low (no interruption)  
- Medium (partial interruption)  
- High (full interruption or data loss)

**Action Taken:**  
Steps taken to contain, resolve, and recover.

**Responsible:**  
Person who handled the incident.

**Status:**  
- Open  
- Under analysis  
- Resolved
