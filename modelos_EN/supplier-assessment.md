## Important Note

This template is a simple tool to help micro and small businesses gather basic information about their suppliers.

It is **not** a formal audit process, nor does it imply that the company has the ability to demand technical requirements from larger suppliers, purchasing groups, or distribution platforms.

The purpose is only to:  
- record minimal available information,  
- increase awareness of risks,  
- and demonstrate reasonable diligence to clients or partners.

If the supplier cannot provide the information, the company may simply record “not applicable” or “information not available”.
